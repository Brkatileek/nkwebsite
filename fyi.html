BIOGRAPHY

 b. January 1991 in Banja Luka, Bosnia and Herzegovina. Working in Berlin, Germany.

He received a painting diploma from the University of Banja Luka's Academy of Arts. Co-founder and participant in the "Apartment" initiative for popularizing contemporary art in Bosnia and Herzegovina, with whom he worked briefly in B&H and the region.

Awarded for artistic work from mixed media research at the Academy of Arts' annual final exhibition in Banja Luka (2014), as well as the award for young video artists in Bosnia and Herzegovina at the "6th NamaTre.ba project/biennale" in collaboration with KulturKontakt Austria and the Austrian federal agency. In 2016 enrolled in the painting MA program at the University of Banja Luka's Academy of Arts.

Meanwhile, he has been a member of the hardcore metal band "Statement of Hate" since 2009. He moved to Germany after being accepted into the master's program "Public Art and New Artist Strategies" at the BAUHAUS University in Weimar. In 2018, he received the "Born to be Bauhaus" award in Weimar. Following his MFA in "Public Art and New Artist Strategies," he moved to Berlin.
