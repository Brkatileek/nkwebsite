FEASIBILITY STUDY 2022
paintings, installation

Paintings, Gouache on paper.
Dimensions 42x33 cm, framed.

The painted scenes depict urban views as the artist is commuting to work through the urban landscape of Berlin that he normally does not physically encounter in his free time. All the images present the same concept and seek an explanation of how the terms "economy" and "death" clash. These are the words most often used in the media of the developed West when discussing the moral and ethical implications of physical isolation and what is known as "lockdown".

"Feasibility studies" are conceptually realized as predetermined thematic-visual representations of the artist's daily commute to work, return home, and the feeling of pressure that the work never ends. The interests of artists along this route can be broken down into four groups that are unique to the development of any feasibility study and depend on the arrangement of the four components: "people, places, power, and processes". From the artist's individual perspective, his dissatisfaction with art institutions during this time period (2020–2023) directed him towards mere economic survival at the expense of artistic "death", in the sense of wasting time or a lack of time for artistic creation.

The artist then decides to start at the very end, positing that all social interactions result in "economy and death," and tries to unwind the narrative backwards, looking for the point of personal functionality. In this light, all paintings become an interstitial emotional compromise between work and artistic activity, a transitional life in liminal space, a compromise between the life and profession of an artist "here and there" in Germany and his homeland, Bosnia and Herzegovina.

AS IF WE ARE FALLING 2023
videos

Videos, duration 7 seconds each.
In process since 2021.
Multiple videos in endlesloop.

AS IF WE ARE FALLING are short Instagram videos of Nikola falling from the sky on his free day from work. He utilizes the shortest video form combined with ritualistic celebration performing the staged laughter after faking the falling from the sky and saying "as if we are falling" with the intent of mocking the present-day and counteracting the complex para-political climate in which he lives, making the auto-ironical statement about social disorientation.
